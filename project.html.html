Webz - Team Project

Our project, Webz, is a web application designed to help small business owners access government funding. After registering, business owners provide proof of address and other required documents. The application then sends this information to the government for approval, and the results are sent back to the business owner. Webz simplifies and speeds up the funding process, making it easier for small businesses to grow and thrive.

Project Features

  • Business owner registration
  • Document submission (proof of address, etc.)
  • Automatic submission to government for approval
  • Notification system for approval status
  • User-friendly interface for small business owners
